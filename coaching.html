Coaching

Together we will acheive incredible things.

Mindi brings a well-rounded approach to behavior change. As a licensed Wellcoach, she works with clients who are seeking more energized lives through mastery of their health; eating habits, nutrition, exercise and stress level. Wellness is about the body, mind and spirit moving forward together.

Embracing who you are and empowering to who you can be. Mindi uses integrative coach tools and has moved hundreds of individuals on their personal journeys to healthier, balanced and more fulfilling lives.

My goal is that you discover your inspiration. By exploring your values and strengths we can create a wellness plan that works within your uniqueness. Change is possible when you have someone to come alongside.
