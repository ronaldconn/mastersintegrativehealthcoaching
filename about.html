Mindi Masters

We Flourish In Life When We Feel Our Best

Mindi brings a well-rounded approach to behavior change. As a licensed Wellcoach, she works with clients who are seeking more energized lives through mastery of their health; eating habits, nutrition, exercise and stress level. Wellness is about the body, mind and spirit moving forward together.

Married and a mother of five, Mindi has maneuvered through the obstacles of fitting self-care into a busy schedule and she understands the challenges each decade of life brings to your health and well being. Along with her consulting practice, she teaches Adult Senior Fitness Classes for Coastline Community College and coaches High School Field Hockey.

Call or email to set up a free 20 minute consultation to determine how we can team together.
